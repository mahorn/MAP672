KENTUCKY'S FOREMOST CITIES

Land of Tobacco Farms, Bourbon and The Kentucky Derby!

Louisville is many things-original, eclectic, historic, dynamic, and energetic. You could spend a lifetime getting to know this beautiful city, the people, the neighborhoods, the culture, and the history that make Louisville truly original. City of Louisville

Kentucky's Capital city nestle between Louisville and Lexington along the Kentucky River is in the heart of bourbon, horse and wine country. The city of Frankfort, is home to several major distilleries of Kentucky Bourbon, including the Buffalo Trace Distillery. City of Frankfort

The Horse Capital of the World, Lexington, is a vibrant city located in the heart of Kentucky's famed scenic Bluegrass Region. Though surrounded by 450 horse farms, Lexington is surprisingly cosmopolitan. Its historic downtown is a compact mixture of art galleries, parks, theaters, restaurants, business offices and charming neighborhoods. City of Lexington
